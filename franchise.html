Business Opportunity to partner with us in our growth.

Telematics, which predominantly covers Asset/Vehicle/Personal Tracking & Monitoring Systems, is the new sunrise industry, which would witness an exponential growth in the next few years. This will be the most sought after product/service in the coming days because of the technology’s inherent ability to solve the current day’s problems related to women and children security, productivity and effective time and resource management.

INDIA is witnessing a tremendous growth in the tracking & monitoring segment, because of the Government’s initiatives in transport related infrastructure and also making it mandatory for Private & Commercial vehicles to have such systems installed.

iTriangle Infotech Pvt Ltd, is a pioneer and an end to end solution provider in this domain. As a Designer, Developer and Manufacturer of GPS-GSM-RFID based Tracking and Monitoring Systems, the Company intends to develop and offer market relevant products and solutions in the domain of Tracking and Monitoring. To keep pace with the exponential growth that the GPS-GSM-RFID based Tracking and Monitoring industry is witnessing, the Company is looking to set its foot print across India and Overseas. To achieve this, iTriangle Infotech intends to have likeminded partners under the Franchisee model who will join hands with the Company & be a part of the winning team to benefit from the growth opportunities that this industry offers. The business proposal offers a stable environment, high growth and a very attractive ROI.

A detailed presentation on the Franchisee Model can be provided to those who are interested to explore this opportunity.
