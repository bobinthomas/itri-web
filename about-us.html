iTriangle Infotech Pvt. Ltd. a Company based at Bangalore in India, is engaged in design, development and manufacture of innovative and next generation products/applications in Telematics.

An ISO 9001-2008 Certified Company

iTriangle has designed & developed a proprietary Remote Data Acquisition Platform based on GSM-GPS-RFID with an end-to-end solution for various sectors like Transport, Logistics, Trading, Educational Institutions, Health Care and the Automobile Industry. The promoters of iTriangle are highly qualified professionals in the related domains. The Company manufactures its products and currently has product/service offerings in the Vehicle Tracking and Monitoring, Fleet Management & Personal Tracking and Safety domains. iTriangle has an end to end platform on telematics which includes, Hardware, firmware and web components which can customized as per market needs and deployed with a less time to market. We have in house capability in Hardware design, Firmware development and web and mobile development which makes us one of the unique companies which can offer OEM/ODM Services to the industry.

Our Telematics platform today consists of below components
Hardware
  • Storage and forward architecture for data
  • RTOS based on micro-kernel architecture.
  • Multi threaded data acquisition, processing, recording and communication
  • Fast and real time data acquisition based on interrupt driver timing and sequencing.
  • Stable and time tested code base.
  • Encryption support for secure communication.
  • Power save and Low power mode.
Firmware
  • Multi threaded port listening application to communicate with remote devices.
  • Supports 1000 concurrent connections with the Remote devices on single port.
  • Optimized for low server latency.
  • Independent database engine to interact with multiple databases.
  • Inbuilt load balancing architecture.
TCP server
  • Multi threaded port listening application to communicate with remote devices.
  • Supports 1000 concurrent connections with the Remote devices on single port.
  • Optimized for low server latency.
  • Independent database engine to interact with multiple databases.
  • Inbuilt load balancing architecture.
Web application
  • Flexible mapping engine.
  • Based on Web2.0 standards.
  • Innovative GUI components for better user interactivity.
  • Strong reporting structures with dynamic quarrying and sorting facility.
  • Innovative design pattern for easy customization.

Product & Technology Highlights

iTriangle has designed & developed a next generation GPS-GSM-RFID based Remote Data Acquisition Platform, which can be productively applied in the domains of Transport, Trading, Logistics, Educational Institutions & Health Care, Defense, Personal Safety, Asset Management etc.

iTriangle has recently launched a Vehicle Tracking and Monitoring Product along with Fleet Management Solution using this platform, under the brand name aQuila. While our product offers highly useful features like tracking, tracing, speed control and reporting, vehicle and driver management, two way communication, route optimization, data and voice communication between the vehicle and the control room; it also offers unique features like accident doubtful point analysis (virtually acts like a black box), fuel pilferage and filling monitor, vehicle immobilization through the back end application. We even provide customized GPS Tracking Solutions for our customers with different functionalities to suite their requirements. It is possible to identify and track some of the events such as theft, accidents, malfunctioning etc. with suitable sensor interface to the tracking device through its unique digital and analog inputs. aQuila Track can accurately locate the geographical coordinate of the assets(truck, car, asset etc.) to which the device is attached, records and/or sends the coordinates along with the events (if any) to a central control room where the information can be plotted on a geographical map.

Other features of Fleet management include Vehicle information like date of purchase, cost, total no of servicing done, life cycle cost and cost incurred during any specified period in terms of maintenance. Servicing alerts including daily servicing. Engine oil refill quantity trend monitoring. Component replacement history, last purchase cost, average failure rate. It should have interface with vehicle monitoring system to detect abnormally high fuel consumption. Job card and completion report generation. The Hardware and the Software for this system is completely developed by us.

As OBD2 is becoming a standardized means of vehicle diagnostic and is becoming a regulation, iTriangle is coming up with a remote vehicle diagnostic platform, integrated with the existing telematics platform. This is going to play a major role in the areas of, Preventive vehicle maintenance, Insurance telematics, connected cars and green automobiles etc.

With the launch of vehicle tracking and fleet management product and with the upcoming vehicle diagnostic platform products, iTriangle establishes itself as a leading player in remote data acquisition, telematics and automotive electronics.

We have customized Fleet Management, Tracking and Monitoring solutions for the domains of Taxi Management, Employee Travel Management, Student transport in Educational Institutions, Sand Mining in Public Works Department, Cash Management Services for Financial Institutions, Muncipal Waste Management for Municipal Corporations.

We have developed a Personal/People Tracking Application, targeted towards tracking and managing the work force of an organization whose people are expected to move around in large geographical areas and a remote monitoring is necessary to ensure productivity. This application can be currently run on any mobile phone which works on an Android OS. We have commenced work to port this Application on an IOS and Windows platform and will be made available shortly.

We have also conceptualized and designed a Multi Parameter Monitoring Health Care Device, using our Remote Data Acquisition Platform, specially targeted towards Cardiac Health Care.

We also have product/service offerings for Personal Tracking and Safety needs, especially for Women, Children, Old aged people and Professional working in high risk sectors. The device under the brand name MeSafe, offers live tracking of individuals, panic button services with SOS, listen in facility, preset alert zones, etc.
