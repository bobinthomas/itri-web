GPS-GSM Vehicle Theft Protection System with Geo Lock. ( For the First Time in India )

This novel Theft Protection System, combines GPS Tracking, Hooter, Immobilizer and Geo Lock, to give its users complete and foolproof security for their Vehicles. Download Details.

Geo Lock

  • On sending a one touch message from your phone, locks the Vehicle in a particular geographical area, where it is being parked.*
  • Activates a hooter alarm if the vehicle crosses the locked geographical area.
  • Sends an alert SMS to the vehicle owner upon Geo Lock Violation.**

Vehicle Immobilizer

  • Immobilizes the vehicle immediately after the vehicle crosses the Geo Locked area.

Locate Vehicle

  • Send SMS to the Mobile connection in the device and receive location information through SMS on your Mobile.

Live/On Demand Tracking

  • Tracks the Vehicle real time and provides location, distance covered, halt/stop info, daily/monthly and Various other reports at an additional cost

Geo Tagging, Geo Fencing, Route Replay features with detailed reports.
